About Me

I am a NASM Certified Personal Trainer. My passion is to help transform peoples' lives through fitness and healthy lifestyle changes.   • Muscle gain

Frequently Asked Questions

Though there are many reasons why you would choose an online personal trainer rather than an in-person, the top reasons that I hear from clients are the affordability, the ability to reach results on their own schedules, the ability to message a personal trainer with any question at any time, and the frequent motivational messages received by the trainer.
Yes! A huge benefit of online personal training is that you can live anywhere and workout anywhere with whatever equipment that you have access to. You will have a fully customizable workout schedule designed to accomplish your fitness goals no matter where you live.
Once you sign up with Coach Kevin Fitness, you will receive a link to our Trainerize profile. Trainerize is a software on both desktop and mobile app that I will use to give you a full workout plan. You will be able to log in and see a calendar that shows all of your workouts scheduled. Once you click on a workout, a rundown of the exercises, goals, reps, sets, and rest times will pop up. There will be a video of each exercise within the workout so that you will know exactly how to perform the exercise with perfect form. When you complete an exercise, you will be able to input and track your progress. The Trainerize software will also allow you to share your daily calories consumption, body weight and progress photos with me so that we can get you to your goals even faster! In return, I will be able to frequently update your workouts in order to adapt to your progress and give you frequent nutrition advice and motivation for when you are outside the gym!
I want to make sure that you know that you are not on this fitness journey alone. We will frequently contact each other via Skype, text, and/or phone call to make sure that you are getting all of the personal relationship benefits of a personal trainer.
Another one of the biggest benefits of having an online personal trainer is affordability. My pricing plan is simple: $150 per month or $400 for 3 months. With your subscription, you will get a fully personalized workout plan for up to 7 workouts per week, suggestions for what you should do on your non-workout days, the ability to message me anytime with your health/fitness related questions, and frequent motivation from me to help kickstart your journey.
